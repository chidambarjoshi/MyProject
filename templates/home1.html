The Internet of Things (IoT) based system and integrated systems i.e. sensors and actuators that allows you to know the groupings of gases in crude milk continuously. Hence, all these sensor technologies, IoT, food science, packaging materials all are combined to come up with a solution that is nothing but smart packaging.

Smart packaging is a technique where sensors are embedded in packages, this technique helps in displaying actual quality & quantity of the product which adds value to the customer safety and increase the shelf life of the product. Smart packaging allows to track the quality of the food product throughout its lifecycle and helps the manufacturer, retailer and consumer to know the condition of the product. Smart packaging technique is used where users will get to know the history of the food product such as manufactured date, expiry date and actual quality of the product in real time.
